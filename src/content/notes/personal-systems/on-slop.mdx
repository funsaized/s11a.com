## What is slop?

Slop is really just a base + toppings. Generally base is one of:

-   Oats (whole, blended).
-   Cream of rice
-   Yogurt (Greek or Skyr)
-   Protein Shake
-   Cottage Cheese
-   Fluffy Yogurt (See tikTok, yogurt + zero sugar jello + baking powder is like frosting fluff) https://www.tiktok.com/t/ZP8xNEVox/

-   Ninja cream nice cream
-   Zero Sugar Jello … b/c volume lol
-   Sometimes sweet potato (mashed or whole)

-   If cooking, do lower and slower, then let it sit out and slowly cool for maximal caramelization. I do like 375 for 1.25 hours then cool

-   Egg Whites

## Some slops fundamentals:

### Bases ingredients I often use:

-   Costco Farmers We know Sprouted Rolled oats

-   **Whole**: mess with the water ratios for overnight for more volume if adding ingredients. Generally follow instructions for ratios when preparing

-   You can make ‘creamier’ protein oats if you whisk in egg whites. This is all over the socials for the ‘technique’ i.e this for inso https://www.tiktok.com/t/ZP8xFAFQe/

-   **Blended:**

-   If doing overnight, don’t put in hot water and it becomes pudding like the next day
-   I use a 6:1 water ratio if doing hot. Pour in some boiling water, whisk a bunch then heat/whisk in 30 second microwave intervals. Doing this then leaving overnight creates a dense ‘gel’ vibe

-   Ie this https://www.instagram.com/reel/DUeG8qmCFrN/?igsh=Y2FqZTQ0bHFqaDRk

-   Sometimes with oats for lower cal I’ll do a ratio of riced / food processed cauliflower

-   Siggis Skyr
-   Zero Sugar Chobani Greek Yogurt
-   Kirkland 0% Greek yogurt
-   Good Culture Cottage Cheese

-   Whole or blended

-   Protein shake

-   I add 1/4 tsp xanthum gum, 1/2 scoop, 200 g ice, about 120g liquid and blend on high for like 2 mins. It activates the gum and you get hella texture and volume. I do this twice for a single scoop

-   Liquids

-   Water
-   MALK almond milk (has to be this brand, no gums/oils)
-   Fairlife milk (2% or whole). Don’t be a loser fat is good for you

-   For ninja creams recipes go on reddit and look up stuff if you want to be healthy or a fatass, depends on ingredients ;)

-   When im healthy I use water + 1/2 protein scoop + xantham gum + guar gum

-   To make things ‘thicker’ I often put in 5/6 grams of Psyllium husk . It’s good fiber. Be careful with ratios as things can get too thick or jelly. But like you can add to greek yogurt + add some liquid (water, milk, almond milk) and double/triple the volume and still get a nice texture

-   Whisk it a lot

Honestly just experiment with bases you like & ratios. I’ll often pick bases based on the amount of cals / carbs I need then go from there to spice it up. Slop is slop you can’t mess it up.
