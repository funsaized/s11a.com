**Black and White Coffee ******

  


**Title****: **Pablo Guerrero 

**About**: 

  * _Origin: _Tangua, Nariño, Colombia
  * _Producer: _Pablo Andres Guerrero 
  * _Process: _Washed_ _
  * _Variety: _Maracuturra (Marogogipe - Caturra hybrid)

**Notes****: **Poached Pear, tinned Peach, Candied Orange, Dark Honey

**Description****:**** **This is a very light roast. 

**Thoughts****: **Clean, vibrant taste. Fruity, sweet, smooth textural experience.

**Grind****: **16/17 size
