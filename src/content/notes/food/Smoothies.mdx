**_Classic Berry Power Smoothie_****__**

_Total: XXX calories | 30g protein___

**_Green Goddess (Vegetable-Forward)_****__**

_Total: 448 calories | 26g protein_

  


**Ingredients:**

**Protein:**

  * 1/2 cup silken tofu (125g) — 94 cal
  * 1 scoop unflavored pea protein (20g) — 80 cal

**Fat:**

  * 1/4 medium avocado (50g) — 60 cal
  * 1 tbsp sunflower seeds (9g) — 51 cal

**Antioxidants:**

  * 2 cups baby spinach (60g) — 14 cal
  * 1/2 cup chopped cucumber (60g) — 8 cal
  * 1/4 cup celery (30g) — 4 cal
  * 2 tbsp fresh parsley (8g) — 3 cal

**Sweetness:**

  * 1/2 medium green apple (90g) — 52 cal
  * 1/4 cup frozen mango (40g) — 25 cal

**Fiber:**

  * From vegetables and fruit — 10g total

**Liquid:**

  * 1 cup coconut water (240mL) — 46 cal
  * 1/2 cup water (120mL) — 0 cal

**Boost:**

  * Juice of 1/2 lime (15mL) — 10 cal
  * 1/4 tsp fresh mint leaves (0.25g) — 1 cal
  * Pinch sea salt (0.1g) — 0 cal

**Macros:**

_Protein: 26g | Fat: 11g | Carbs: 28g | Fiber: 10g___

  


**Preparation Instructions**

**Blending Order:**

1. Add liquids first

2. Add psyllium husk and let sit 30 seconds

3. Add soft ingredients (banana, dates/mango, nut butters)

4. Add protein powder and yogurt

5. Add remaining ingredients

6. Blend on high for 60-90 seconds until smooth

**Tips:**

• Best consumed immediately

• Can refrigerate up to 24 hours (shake before drinking)

• Start blender on low, increase to high speed

• Add extra liquid if too thick
